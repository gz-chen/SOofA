NAME
	main_genDesign.bin - generate some stratum OofA designs
	
SYNOPSIS
	./main_genDesign.bin COMPONENT STRENGTH REDUCTION SYMBPERM SAVE
	COAgenerator
	
DESCRIPTION
	This programme can produce some of the known stratum OofA designs
	proposed in the ARTICLE "Stratum order-of-addition designs"
	by Liushan Zhou, Ze Liu, Min-Qian Liu and Guanzhou Chen.
	
ARGUMENTS
	COMPONENT
		The number of components of the design.
		It should be a positive integer.
		
	STRENGTH
		The desired strength of stratum orthogonality.
		
	REDUCTION
		The ratio between the numbers of runs
		of the generated design and a component orthogonal array of the same strength.
		
	SYMBPERM
		This argument should be 1.
		
	SAVE
		If SAVE is 1, then the generated design will be printed to a .txt file in "./des/";
		otherwise, the design will not be saved in a file.
		
EXAMPLE
	To produce the design X in Example 1 of ARTICLE
	and save it to "./des/OofAdes_10_5_2_2_1.txt":
		./main_genDesign.bin 5 2 2 1 1
		
	To produce the design X-hat in Example 5 of ARTICLE
	and save it to "./des/OofAdes_60_6_3_2_1.txt":
		./main_genDesign.bin 6 3 2 1 1
		
	To produce the design X in Example 6 of ARTICLE
	and save it to "./des/OofAdes_28_8_2_2_1.txt":
		./main_genDesign.bin 8 2 2 1 1
		
	To produce the design X in Example S6 of ARTICLE and do not save it to a file:
		./main_genDesign.bin 8 2 4 1 0
		
	To produce the design X-hat with 840 runs in Example S12 of ARTICLE
	and do not save it to a file:
		./main_genDesign.bin 7 4 2 1 0
		
	To produce the design X-hat with 60 runs in Example S13 of ARTICLE
	and do not save it to a file:
		./main_genDesign.bin 10 2 3 1 0
